John Conway's Game of Life

Cellular Life Automaton consists in a collection of cells, which based on a few mathematical rules, can live, die or multiply, depending on the initial conditions you create.

LightMorse
  • iPhone

Morse code in your pocket

During your hikes, or general adventures, you may find yourself in a dangerous position without the possibility to contact anyone. LightMorse was created with that in mind.

Create custom messages or use the default ones and convert any given text into morse code, that will be used by your phone's flashlight to signal someone at long ranges.
